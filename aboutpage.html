All About Midnights

General Album Info:

Midnights was released on October 21, 2022. The standard version consists of 13 tracks:

1. Lavender Haze
2. Maroon
3. Anti-Hero
4. Snow on the Beach
5. You're on Your Own Kid
6. Midnight Rain
7. Question...?
8. Vigilante Sh*t
9. Bejeweled
10. Labyrinth
11. Karma
12. Sweet Nothing
13. Mastermind

The album was an instant success. Within less than 24 hours, it broke the record for the most streamed album on Spotify within a single day.
Not only that, but Swift became the first artist in history to simultaneously hold all top 10 spots on the Billboard Hot 100. (Pictured Below)
